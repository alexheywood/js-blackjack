Beat The Dealer.

You start with two cards. The aim of the game is to have a hand as high as possible without exceeding 21.

To begin you and the dealer are dealt two cards.

You can "add a card" to your hand (as many times as you want) or you can "stick" to end your turn and allow the dealer to draw one card before revealing both hands.

You win if your hand is closer to 21 or if the dealer's hand exceeds 21.

You lose if your hand exceeds 21 or if the dealer's hand is closer to 21.
